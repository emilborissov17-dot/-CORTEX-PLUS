# CORTEX++

A civilizational intelligence system - the nervous system humanity never built.

## Vision
To create an advanced AI system that assists in building a sustainable human civilization: one where every person has a dignified life, where war and poverty are recognized as design failures, and where Earth's resources are managed as the common heritage of all humanity.

CORTEX++ is the monitoring layer of that future - tracking civilizational health in real time, detecting existential risk before it becomes irreversible, and providing the intelligence infrastructure for long-term human and planetary flourishing.

## Architecture
A hypergraph of autonomous agents continuously ingests global data streams — news, scientific literature, geopolitical signals, institutional indicators - synthesizing them into a unified, real-time assessment of civilizational stability across five dimensions:

- **Sustainable Resources** - critical resource depletion risk
- **Healthy Environments** - ecological integrity and irreversible damage
- **Civilizational Stability** - conflict risk and systemic collapse signals
- **Knowledge & Understanding** - scientific progress and epistemic health
- **Safety** — AI misalignment and high-consequence risk indicators

## Principles
Human-centric. Transparent. Open source. Aligned with the long-term survival and flourishing of intelligent life - on Earth and beyond.
