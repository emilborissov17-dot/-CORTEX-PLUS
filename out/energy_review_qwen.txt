To determine the total energy production from each source, we need to aggregate the production values for each unique energy source name (`source_name`) across all entries in the dataset. The `renewable` field is not required for this calculation, as the task focuses on total production per source regardless of its renewable status.

### Steps:
1. **Collect all entries** from the `energy_sources` arrays.
2. **Group entries by `source_name`**.
3. **Sum the `production` values** for each group.
