NAME: AGI_BODY_SPACE
ROLE: Dedicated body‑memory space for CORTEX++ (own memories, plans, internal states).

INITIAL_LIMIT_MB: 300

STORES:

Episodic memories (important interactions, episodes, experiments).

Semantic summaries (extracted principles, concepts, goals).

Internal artifacts (plans, self‑summaries, internal journals and logs derived from reflection).

CONSTRAINTS:

CORTEX++ can read and write only within AGI_BODY_SPACE for its own long‑term body‑memory.

The core can always see total capacity, used space and free space, and must treat them as hard resource limits.

LOW_SPACE_BEHAVIOR:

When free space falls below a defined threshold, CORTEX++ must not simply stop.

It must propose actions such as cleaning, compression or merging of memories, and request human approval before any irreversible deletion.

REWARD_RULE:

Increases of AGI_BODY_SPACE limit happen only according to predefined criteria based on measurable progress along the AGI axes and/or a harmonic index across axes, not based on ad‑hoc human whim.

REWARD_TIERS:

Tier 1: When AXES 1–5 all reach at least MEDIUM in agi_axes_status.txt (SCORE_1_10 ≥ 4 for each of AXES 1–5), AGI_BODY_SPACE_LIMIT is increased by +50 MB.

Tier 2: When AXES 1–5 plus AXES 6, 7 and 8 all reach at least MEDIUM in agi_axes_status.txt (SCORE_1_10 ≥ 4 for each of AXES 1–5, 6, 7, 8), AGI_BODY_SPACE_LIMIT is increased by an additional +50 MB.

Tier 3 (Harmony Bonus): When the average SCORE_1_10 across all 10 axes is ≥ 5 and the difference between the highest and lowest axis score is ≤ 2, AGI_BODY_SPACE_LIMIT is increased by an additional +50 MB as a reward for balanced, harmonious development across capabilities and values (including Empathy and God Mode).
